THE SEASON

It has been long awaited by many yet, only a few get to feel it's warmth.
How sad it is not to be here, now that it is finally here.
But taking in the warmth of it,allowing it to heal our wounds and taking away the pain and the sorrows, and wiping away the tears.
Oh, how good it feels when we allow the joy, peace, and love of our LORD to fill our hearts.
By doing this good to ourselves, we can then extend a helping hand to others.
Filling them with the very joy, peace, and love that is overflowing in our own hearts, that the SAVIOUR has filled us with.
It's time to embrace the love of JESUS , as HE is being born for us once again.
Yes, it's the season of joy, peace, and, above all LOVE, for it is the love for mankind that CHRIST was born.

THE SEASON OF LOVE is here again!!!
